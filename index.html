About
Thanks for your interest! I am a PhD student at Computer Science Department at Yale University, where I enjoy the privilege to work at LILY lab with Professor Dragomir Radev. My research interest is Deep Learning and Natural Language Processing. Before coming to Yale, I was a graduate student at Computer Science and Engineering Department at the University of Michigan. I also got my bachelor degrees at both University of Michigan and Shanghai Jiao Tong University from the UM-SJTU Joint Institute.
